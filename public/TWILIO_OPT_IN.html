SMS Double Opt-In Process

This page documents the double opt-in process used when customers sign up to receive SMS communications from [Company Name]. This process ensures compliance with telecommunications regulations and provides a clear record of consent.

1) Initial Opt-In Request

Message Template:
Hi from [Company Name]! Reply YES to this message to confirm you want to receive SMS updates. Msg&Data rates may apply.

2) Confirmation Message

Message Template:
Thanks for confirming! You'll now receive marketing and alert messages from [Company Name]. Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe.

This page is hosted on our public site as proof of our consent flow for Twilio A2P registration. The company name shown in actual SMS messages will be replaced with the specific business sending the communication.
